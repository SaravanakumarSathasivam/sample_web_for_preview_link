privacy-notice-deposits

This Privacy Notice (“Notice”) is intended for prospective and current UnionDigital Deposit Account Customers, whether individual or corporate. This shall include individuals who have provided their information or documents for the opening of an individual or as a representative in a corporate UnionDigital Deposit Account, whether the application was approved or rejected. Furthermore, this Notice applies to individuals who utilize our various products and services, which includes, but is not limited to, making bill payments, transferring funds, availing time deposits, and obtaining and using our virtual debit cards, both as retail customers and as representatives of corporate account holders.
